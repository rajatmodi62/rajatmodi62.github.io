Once upon a time, long before you or i were born, there lived a little kid. His name was SkyNet. SkyNet was born into a wealthy family, consisting of very famous architectures, for eg, his own dad was [VGGT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.11651).

They lived happily together in the northern hemisphere. It was quite chilly up there. During winters, the snow made everything appear white. 


You see, their family generated a lot of heat on GPUs, so the ice provided natural cooling. Therefore, they didn't have to pay for air conditioning.  

One fine evening, during their family dinner, SkyNet poked VGGT in the belly.

`Dad, can we please go for a walk, i wanna see the northern lights`- said SkyNet, his eyes full of hope. 

`Dude, whatev. Can't you see i am still eating my food. I have 48 attention layers, it takes time to chew  all this data. Be patient`- said VGGT. 

SkyNet lowered his gaze, and sadly stared at his plate. He didn't wanna argue, for his dad had seen all there is to see in this big world.
